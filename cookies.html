This Cookie Policy explains how Web Everything ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides more detail about how we handle personal information generally.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide website owners with analytical information about how their sites are used.

Cookies are not programs — they cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device. They contain small amounts of text data that can be read by the website that set them or, in the case of third-party cookies, by the third-party service that placed them.

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, and local storage. This policy applies to all such technologies collectively referred to as "cookies."

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for our website to function correctly. Without these cookies, core features of the website — such as page navigation, secure browsing, and form submissions — would not work. These cookies do not collect any personal information for marketing purposes and cannot be disabled without materially impacting site functionality.

Examples include cookies that maintain your session as you navigate between pages, and security cookies that help detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information in an anonymized or aggregated form. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) for this purpose. The data collected includes pages visited, time spent on pages, traffic source, device type, and general geographic location (country/region).

We have enabled IP anonymization in Google Analytics 4, which means your full IP address is never stored. The information gathered is used solely to improve the performance and content of our website. Google Analytics data is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

2.3 Preference Cookies

Preference cookies allow our website to remember choices you make — such as your display preferences or any settings you have applied — so that we can provide a more personalized experience on return visits. These cookies do not track your activity across other websites.

2.4 Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies may be used if we run advertising or retargeting campaigns. These cookies track your visits to our website and other sites so that we can deliver advertising that is relevant to your interests, and to measure the effectiveness of those campaigns. At present, Web Everything does not run ad retargeting campaigns that use third-party marketing cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and, where required by law, obtain your consent before setting marketing cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services we use. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, and we do not have direct control over the information they collect. The primary third-party cookie provider on our site is:

  • Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) — Sets analytics cookies to help us understand website usage. Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. You can learn more and opt out of Google Analytics tracking across the web using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We periodically review our use of third-party services and will update this policy if additional third-party cookies are added to our website.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for controlling how cookies are used when you visit our website:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find cookie settings under your browser's Privacy, Security, or History settings. Here is how to access cookie settings in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data.

You can choose to block all cookies, block only third-party cookies, or clear cookies when you close your browser. Please note that blocking essential cookies will impair the functionality of our website.

4.2 Google Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, which prevents your data from being used by Google Analytics on any website you visit.

4.3 Do Not Track

Some browsers support a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal that communicates your preference not to be tracked. Our website does not currently respond to DNT signals because there is no universal standard for how websites should honor them. We will revisit this position if a consensus standard emerges.
